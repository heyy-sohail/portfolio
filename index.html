About me

Hey, I'm Sohail Azain a developer who genuinely enjoys building things for the web. I got into coding because I liked the idea that you could make something out of nothing, and that feeling hasn't really gone away.

Over time I've picked up a pretty broad stack HTML, CSS, and JavaScript are where I spend most of my time, but I'm also comfortable with Python, Java, SQL, and MongoDB on the backend, and I've been building mobile apps with React Native and Angular too.

I care a lot about how things actually feel to use, not just how they look in a screenshot. Every project I work on, I try to leave it a little better than I found it — whether that's cleaner code, a smoother interaction, or just something that's genuinely nice to use.
